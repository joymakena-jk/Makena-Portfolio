ABOUT

Hi, I'm Kagwiria.

I'm a front-end developer based in Nairobi, building responsive websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I like taking a brand's world — its colors, its mood, what it's trying to say — and turning that into a working site with real interactivity, not just a static layout.

Every project on this page was built from scratch, without page builders or templates. I care as much about the code being clean and accessible as I do about the design looking intentional.
